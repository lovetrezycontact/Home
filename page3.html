тЬи How to Make the Secret-Message Valentine Card (English)

Materials

  • Colored papers (red/pink/white)
  • Scissors
  • Glue / double-sided tape
  • Ruler
  • Pencil
  • Black marker/pen
  • Small stickers/hearts (optional)

Steps

  1. Make the card base тАУ Cut a rectangle of colored paper and fold it in half to form the card.
  2. Create a big heart тАУ Cut a large heart from colored paper for the front (or inside) decoration.
  3. Add a flap/pocket тАУ Score a light fold on one side of the heart and glue only the edges to form a small pocket or flap that can lift/open.
  4. Prepare the secret message тАУ Cut a thin strip (or small note), write your hidden message, and fold it (flat or accordion).
  5. Hide the message тАУ Slide the message strip into the pocket/under the flap; leave a tiny tab visible and draw a little тАЬPull тЖТтАЭ arrow.
  6. Decorate тАУ Add small hearts, borders, and cute doodles around the heart; outline with a marker for a clean finish.
  7. Finish & test тАУ Open/close the flap to check the reveal; write a greeting inside the card.
