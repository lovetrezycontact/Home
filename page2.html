тЬи How to Make This Cute Pop-Up Gift Card (English)

Materials Needed

  • Colored craft paper
  • Scissors
  • Glue
  • Marker/pen

Steps

  1. Take a rectangular colored paper and fold it in half to form a card.
  2. Cut out a teddy bear shape (or use printed cut-outs) and decorate it with small details.
  3. Inside the card, create a folded pop-up strip and attach the teddy bear cut-out to it.
  4. Add small hearts, stars, or other shapes around the teddy bear for decoration.
  5. On the card cover, paste decorative cut-outs and draw cute borders with a pen.
  6. Write your personal message inside the card to complete your pop-up gift.

Your pop-up gift card is ready ЁЯОЙ тАУ sweet, simple, and full of love!
